Analytical Development Laboratory: We maintain a well-designed department equipped with modern instruments and qualified analysts to support product development through analytical method development and transfer activities.

Formulation & Development (F&D): Our team focuses on developing stable, cost-effective products using a "concept-to-design" approach. We ensure smooth technology transfer from pre-formulation to final stability studies in compliance with ICH guidelines.

Regulatory Approvals & Compliance: Our facility is FDCA (Gujarat) approved and GMP compliant. We are regularly inspected and approved by global regulatory authorities, ensuring we meet international standards.

Manufacturing & Operations: Operations are supervised by qualified professionals under experienced management. We procure DMF-grade materials only from approved vendors to ensure high quality and regulatory compliance.

Storage & Warehousing: We maintain dedicated, climate-controlled warehouses to ensure the safe storage of raw materials and finished products until distribution.

Engineering & Utilities: Our experienced engineering team manages critical utilities, including purified water, HVAC systems, steam, and compressed air, ensuring full compliance with regulatory guidelines.

Quality Control (QC) Laboratory: Equipped with modern instruments, our in-house QC lab performs rigorous testing to guarantee product purity, safety, and efficacy, delivering timely and accurate results.

Quality Assurance (QA): Our QA team monitors every stage of manufacturing and distribution. Products are released only after thorough review. QA also oversees validation, stability studies, and regular facility inspections.

Regulatory Affairs: Our experienced regulatory team keeps the company updated on global regulations and provides technical support for generating and compiling quality dossiers.

Manufacturing Utility

  • Separate Air Handling Unit (AHU) for each zone. Total 9 AHUs are installed at our facility. Each of the primary packaging department has their own separate AHUs.
  • In-House purified water plant for Reverse Osmosis Water & Demineralized Water.
  • In house, we have fully equipped Physiochemical laboratory.
  • In house, we have fully equipped Microbiology laboratory.
  • ETP installed as per Gujarat Pollution Control Board (GPCB).
  • Air-Curtains & PVC curtains are Installed at required entries to prevent air-contamination.
  • Separate Dispensing Booths for API & Excipients to avoid cross contamination.
  • Air Conditioning is provided wherever required i.e. R.M. Storage, P.M. Storage, Secondary Packaging, Retain Sample and Instrument Lab etc.
  • Separate defined areas for Each Critical Processing Zones to avoid cross contamination.
  • Air compression with moisture separator.
  • An Elevator for handling materials.
  • Our plant is fully installed with fire extinguisher.
  • 24 hours security.
