Our Capabilities & Quality Systems

Analytical Development Laboratory (ADL)

In support of Formulation & Development (F&D), RareBee Lifesciences maintains a well-designed Analytical Development department equipped with modern analytical instruments and staffed by a team of qualified analysts. The ADL supports the development function by performing analytical method development and method transfer activities.

Formulation & Development (F&D)

The F&D team primarily focuses on developing products that are stable, cost-effective, and based on an optimized process. We emphasize smooth technology transfer with a "concept-to-design" approach right from pre-formulation studies to final stability studies, all conducted as per ICH guidelines.

Products are transferred for higher-scale manufacturing by following approved technology transfer procedures, with thorough evaluation of product development parameters and analytical development data.

Regulatory Approvals & Compliance

Our facility is approved by FDCA (Gujarat State) and complies with GMP standards. It has also been inspected and approved by regulatory authorities from various worldwide markets, with regular inspections conducted at defined intervals.

Manufacturing & Operations

Manufacturing, packaging, and materials handling operations are supervised and controlled by qualified, competent, and trained team members under the guidance of a highly experienced management team.

Materials used for manufacturing and packing activities are procured only from approved vendors, using quality-based DMF-grade materials as per regulatory requirements and norms.

Storage & Warehousing

The company maintains its own dedicated warehouses for the safe and effective storage of raw materials and finished products, with required control of temperature and humidity (as applicable) until distribution.

Quality Control (QC) Laboratory

Our QC lab is equipped with modern instruments to perform in-house analytical tests, ensuring assurance and guarantee of product purity, safety, and efficacy. The in-house QC lab delivers timely and accurate results as part of rigorous product quality checks.

Quality Assurance (QA)

Our QA team continuously monitors and controls predefined product specifications at all stages of manufacturing, storage, and distribution. Products are released for market distribution only after QA approval, following a thorough review of manufacturing documents, analytical results, and comparison against approved specifications.

QA plays a vital role in process validation, product stability studies, cleaning validation, market complaint handling, and self-inspection of the facility at regular intervals.

Regulatory Affairs

The Regulatory team at RareBee Lifesciences forms the core strength of the company, backed by highly experienced and qualified members. They keep all departments updated on regulatory changes, prevalent regulations, and provide technical support to generate and compile quality dossiers.
