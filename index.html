About Us

With humble beginnings in 2003 as a wholesale pharma distributor in Gujarat, Rarebee Lifesciences started as a pharma company in 2021 with 15 products and has since grown to over 150 finished products in the market. By 2026, Rarebee has established its first pharma unit, Rarebee Pharma Pvt. Ltd.

Rarebee Lifesciences is one of the most promising and fast-growing companies in the global healthcare landscape. Established in 2021, we have built a strong foundation rooted in innovation, quality, and trust, delivering high-performance pharmaceutical products that improve lives and support healthier communities.

With a proven legacy of excellence, Rarebee group continues to set benchmarks in providing reliable and effective healthcare solutions. Our team dedicated professionals drives our consistent year-on-year growth, reflecting our commitment to sustainable progress and positive global impact.

Facility

The Rarebee Group has its own manufacturing facility named Rarebee Pharma Pvt. Ltd. Rarebee Pharma manufactures syrups and ointments, and it is also planning to produce capsules and tablets.

The facility is approved by the FDCA (Gujarat) under revised Schedule M and it is GMP certified. It has also applied for WHO certification.

All the products are developed by expert pharmacist with required evaluation of drug product molecule for mainly oral solid dosage forms and other dosage forms.

Rarebee Pharma Pvt. Ltd. offers third-party manufacturing and contract manufacturing services, enabling you to develop your own brand products.

Global Presence

With a vision to expand further and make healthcare accessible to all, Rarebee Group is dedicated to building lasting relationships with our global partners. Together, we're shaping the future of healthcare.

Rarebee Lifesciences collaborates with numerous WHO GMP certified pharmaceutical, cosmetic, nutraceutical, and surgical manufacturers, all equipped with complete export documentation, to serve global markets.

Rarebee Group offers contract and third-party manufacturing services for the global market. This enables our international partners to create their own branded products with custom packaging, allowing them to easily market and sell under their own brand names.

Rarebee Group fulfills all product demands for global partners, providing complete documentation and dossier processes as needed. This is enabled by our association with certified manufacturing plants.
