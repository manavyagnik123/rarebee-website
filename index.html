About Us

With humble beginnings in 2003 as a wholesale pharma distributor in Gujarat, Rarebee Lifesciences started as a pharma company in 2021 with 15 products and has since grown to over 150 finished products in the market. By 2026, Rarebee has established its first pharma unit, Rarebee Pharma Pvt. Ltd.

Rarebit Lifesciences is one of the most promising and fast-growing companies in the global healthcare landscape. Established in 2021, we have built a strong foundation rooted in innovation, quality, and trust, delivering high-performance pharmaceutical products that improve lives and support healthier communities.

With a proven legacy of excellence, Rarebee group continues to set benchmarks in providing reliable and effective healthcare solutions. Our team dedicated professionals drives our consistent year-on-year growth, reflecting our commitment to sustainable progress and positive global impact.

Facility

The Rarebee Group has its own manufacturing facility named Rarebee Pharma Pvt. Ltd. Rarebee Pharma manufactures syrups and ointments, and it is also planning to produce capsules and tablets.

All the products are developed by expert pharmacist with required evaluation of drug product molecule for mainly oral solid dosage forms and other dosage forms.
