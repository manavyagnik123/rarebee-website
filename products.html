Rarebee Lifesciences - Domestic

Rarebee Lifesciences was established in 2021 as a Propaganda Cum Distribution (PCD) Pharma Company in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. It offers approximately 150+ finished products in the domestic market, spanning a range of antibiotics, tablets, capsules, oral liquids, ointments, and injectables. All products are manufactured in WHO-GMP certified units and adhere to strict quality assurance standards.

Rarebee Lifesciences - Export

Rarebee Lifesciences delivers third-party manufacturing products to international partners, providing fully finished goods complete with all required export documentation and rigorous quality assurance.


We partner with multiple WHO-GMP certified plants specializing in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, nutraceuticals, and surgical products all are fully equipped for global exports with comprehensive documentation.


As your one-stop solution, Rarebee Lifesciences simplifies sourcing, ensuring seamless quality and compliance for partners worldwide.

Rarebee Pharma Pvt. Ltd. - third party Manufacturing

Rarebee Pharma Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturing unit under the Rarebee Group, specializing in oral liquids and ointments. We provide third-party and contract manufacturing services to clients across the industry.


Our state-of-the-art plant is approved under Revised Schedule M, GMP certified, and has WHO certification in process. We maintain the highest quality standards with our in-house micro lab and QC Lab.
